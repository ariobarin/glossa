What it is

A thin bridge, not another agent.

Glossa does not add a model, planner, or conversation store. It gives ChatGPT access to one workspace that you explicitly expose from your Windows computer.

The local worker owns file containment and command execution. The hosted relay handles authentication and delivery. Nothing clones your repository into the relay.
